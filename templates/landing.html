Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TranscriptHub

What types of YouTube videos can I extract transcripts from?

You can extract transcripts from any YouTube video that has captions or subtitles available. This includes auto-generated captions, which most YouTube videos have. The system works with videos in any language that has available captions.

How accurate is the AI in answering questions about video content?

Our AI assistant is powered by Mistral AI, one of the most advanced language models available. It analyzes the transcript and provides accurate, relevant answers based on the video content. While the AI is highly sophisticated, it's important to note that it can only work with information present in the transcript.

Are my transcripts and conversations private?

Yes, your data privacy is important to us. All your transcripts and conversations are securely stored and are only accessible to you through your account. We do not share your data with third parties or use it for purposes other than providing our service to you.

Can I download or export the transcripts?

Yes, you can download any transcript as a text file. This feature is available on all plans, including the free tier. This makes it easy to use the transcript content in other applications or for your research purposes.

What if a YouTube video doesn't have captions?

TranscriptHub can only extract transcripts from videos that have captions available, either manually added or auto-generated. If a video doesn't have any form of captions, our system will not be able to extract a transcript. Most YouTube videos have auto-generated captions, but some very recent uploads or videos with specific settings may not have them yet.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period.
